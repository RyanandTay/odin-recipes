Pigs in a Puff Pastry Blanket

Completed Batch of Delicious Pigs in Blanket

This recipe includes the detailed preparation steps required to make the most delicious batch of Pigs in a Puff Pastry Blanket you have ever tried!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb pkg frozen puff pastry flour, for dusting
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 8 uncooked jumbo all-beef kosher hot dogs, cut in 1/2 lenght wise
  • 2 TBS smoky or grainy mustard
  • Small handful of herbs, such as thyme or rosemary
  • Kosher salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400F
  2. Defrost puff pastry according to pkg instructions
  3. Roll the pastry out on a floured counter to a rectangle about 19x12"
  4. Cut the large recrtangle in 1/2 lengthwise
  5. Cut both smaller rectangles into eight equal sections down to 16 total
