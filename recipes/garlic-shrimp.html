Garlic Shrimp Asparagus Skillet

Perfectly cooked Garlic Shrimp Asparagus Skillet

This recipe will show you how to make irresistible Garlic Shrimp Asparagus Skillet

Ingredients

  • 1 lb uncooked extra-large shrimp
  • kosher salt and fresh ground pepper
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 3 TBS butter/olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups mushrooms
  • 1 bunch asparagus
  • 1 TBS parsley

Steps

  1. In a bowl, add the shrimp, salt, pepper, red pepper flakes, and onion powder. Mix everythin well.
  2. In a skillet, add 2 TBS butter or olive oil over medium heat.
  3. Add the garlic, add saute for 30 sec. Add the shrimp and saute for about 4 min or until the shrimp are cooked through. Set aside.
  4. In the same skillet, add 1 TBS of olive oil or butter
